Mantra Merchandise

According to Wikipedia, a mantra is a sound, syllable, word, or group of words that is considered capable of “creating transformation.”

For this assignment we chose a personal mantra, then design ed and produced an actual merchandise item to showcase it. The Mantra I chose was“Inhale, Exhale”which is something I often tell myself in stressfull situations. The shape and compostion of the design represents the pattern of deep breathing, where our lungs expand and contract. The mantra was placed onto a notebook, which is where I write down my feelings and release my anxiety.
