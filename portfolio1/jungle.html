Concrete Jungle

Medium: Recycled Material Art: CD's, garbage bags etc.

I created this Installation after my house underwent a clean up to remove the excess clutter. I was shocked seeing the pile of waste collected & the amount of hazardous e-waste and plastics. This reminded me of the materialistic mindset the world has come to. We buy and hoard, never to give it a thought about its effect on the environment. Hence, I recycled these scraps to craft this piece. In the top of the dress, I use e-waste like old bulbs, CDâ€™s, Earphones and cables as budding flowers and buds to show a jungle scene. This portrays how the society's industries have shifted our priorities in our lives. Aluminum wire weaves the entire structure & acts as the stems that the metal leaves spring from. The train of the gown depicts a river of rubbish emerging from the concrete jungle- made of old garbage bags and soda cans symbolic of the excess waste produced by us. Lastly, placing a withered leaf in the middle as a warning &reminder that nature is dying between the battle of materials.
