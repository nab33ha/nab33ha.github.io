WikiBook: Kanye West

Using Wikipedias own list of longest pages , I compiled and designed a book. Carefully considering what it means to translate information from one medium to another, I explored what it means to translate the ideas within the content into visual form. How can the functions that we have come to rely on in Wikipedia be represented in print?

For this project, the topic I chose was the American singer, rapper and producer Kanye West. The Book encapsulates his music career and Album discography from the begining of his career till present day. The book is meant to be visually and typographically engaging and refreshing. As you flip through each chapter, the pages reflects the style, fashion and vibe of the artists album of that particular year. Kanye west is acclaimed for his music as well as his online persona and outspoken social media handles including twitter, the chapters touch upon these quirks and jests through a Tweet of the Year page in every chapter as a break from the reading.
