The Money Plant

Medium: Material Art; Aluminum wire, Printed paper

I pondered why almost every Indian household had a money plant. After asking several families, I discovered a common Asian superstition - the money plant is said to bring prosperity to the home. Hence, its name. Realizing the true reason this plant was present in my home, I reconstructed it. Using printed money bills of different currency as leaves and aluminium wire bent to form stems, I highlighted the unfortunate importance of wealth over nature in this materialistic and money-driven society.
