Manifesto Poster

Whilst ideating who to choose for my manifesto , I knew I wanted to represent my heritage and choose an Indian social and political figure to explore the history of my home country. Therefore, I chose Rabindranath Tagore,an Indian Poet polymath, painter and social reformer. Exploring the use of typography, grid, color, shape, and scale, the aim was to create a compelling composition. The poster intends to offer a vivid expression of the content at hand and an insight into my perspective towards it. Considering the manipulation, presentation, intervention, and expression, I used typography as my primary visual language and image.
