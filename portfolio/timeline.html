Imagine, Interpret, Illustrate

This is an experimental design project wherein the user has to interpret and re-imagine colorful paint blobs. Upon hovering over the empty blob, uncovers my interpretation of the shape through my illustration. Much similar to the game where people try and make meaning out of clouds in the sky, this guessing game invokes creativity and curiosity within the user. This is also one of the first projects where I was introduced to Javascript and included it towards the end where the user can themselves draw on the paint blobs and illustrate.
